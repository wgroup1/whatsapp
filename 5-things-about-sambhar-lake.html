Top 5 Things About Sambhar Lake


Hello friends, today we will know about Sambhar Lake. Sambhar Lake is fifty kilometers away from Jaipur. There are many options to reach Sambhar from Jaipur. You can also go to Sambhar by bus, train and private vehicles.


Why Sambhar Lake is Famous


By the way, Sambhar Lake is famous due to the salt water lake, but friends, there is a very old temple here of Shakambhari Devima.


5 Things About Sambhar Lake


No-1. Asia's largest saltwater lake

Sambhar Lake is Asia's largest saltwater lake, it is spread over 50 square kilometers.


No-2. Largest place for migratory birds in Rajasthan


Sambhar Lake is the biggest place for migratory birds in Rajasthan, lakhs of birds come here every year from abroad and stay back during the rainy days.


No-3. Bollywood movie making special place


Friends, you might not know, but a lot of Bollywood movies are shot here and a lot of Hindi songs are also made here, Bollywood's famous movies like - Jodha Akbar, Veer, Dehli - 7, Pk etc have made a lot of movies here.


No-4.Sakhmbhari Mata Temple


Friends, here is the very old and grand temple of Sakhambhari Mata.


No-5.India's largest salt producing region


India's largest food salt producing area is Sambhar Lake.
